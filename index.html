Welcome to Egg Master

Egg Master is a simple, open-source app designed to help you boil the perfect egg every time. Whether you like your eggs soft, medium, or hard, Egg Master has you covered!

Features

  • Customizable timers for soft, medium, and hard-boiled eggs
  • Simple, user-friendly interface
  • Open-source and free to use
