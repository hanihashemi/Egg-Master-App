Thank you for using Egg Master! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Data Collection

Egg Master does not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal data. The app is designed to operate without requiring user data.

2. Third-Party Services

Egg Master integrates with third-party services, specifically:

  • Firebase: Used for essential app functionality.
  • Google Cloud: Used for backend processes.

These third-party services may process generic usage information for operational purposes. However, Egg Master does not collect or store data through these services.

For more information, please review their privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud Privacy Policy

3. Data Storage and Retention

Egg Master does not store any user data on devices or external servers. Any information processed by third-party services is not accessible to or retained by us.

4. Permissions

To ensure optimal functionality, Egg Master requests the following permissions:

  • android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Allows the app to operate tasks in the foreground.
  • android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to notify users about boiling progress.
  • android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE: Enables specific features related to boiling timers.

These permissions are utilized solely for the intended functionalities of the app and not for collecting any user data.
